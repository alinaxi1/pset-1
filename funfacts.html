FUN FACTS!

  1. Lions have the loudest roar of all the big cats. A male's roar can travel 8 km.
  2. Lionesses need 5 kg of meat per day. Males need 7 kg of meat per day.
  3. Cubs can also be called whelp or lionet.
  4. A lion's tongue is rough enough to skin a human by licking them.
  5. Lionesses prefer darker manes to lighter ones. The most popular theory for this is that darker manes indicate a healthier lion.
